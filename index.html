Stan Lee

Excelsior!

The American comic book writer, editor, publisher, and producer; Stan Lee.

Stan Lee was born on December 28, 1922, in Manhattan, New York City. Lee became an assistant in 1939 at the new Timely Comics division of Martin Goodman's company. By the 1960s, the company would evolve into Marvel Comics. The first superheroes Lee and artist Jack Kirby created together were the Fantastic Four, which became immediately popular. After many years and character developments, Lee became a figurehead and public face for Marvel Comics. Sadly, he passed away on November 12, 2018. Stan Lee's legacy lives on.

Lee co-created numerous fictional characters, including:

  • Fantastic Four
  • Hulk
  • Thor
  • Iron Man
  • X-Men
  • Dardevil
  • Doctor Strange
  • Spiderman
